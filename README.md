# Insights
1. Profitability Analysis
Profit by Category: Tracks the performance of Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology over four years. Technology consistently shows strong profit margins across different periods.

Top 5 Customers by Profit: Identifies the most valuable clients, led by Tamara Chand, followed by Raymond Buch and Sanjit Chand.

2. Sales Trends & Distribution
Sales by Month: A line chart reveals seasonal peaks, with significant performance spikes in March ($205,006) and December ($241,464).

Sales by Sub-Category: Utilizes a funnel visualization to show that Phones and Chairs are the primary drivers of sales volume.

Geographic Sales: A map chart visualizes sales distribution across US states, highlighting high-revenue regions like California and New York.

3. Customer Growth
Customer Count by Year: A donut chart tracks the expansion of the customer base from 2021 to 2024, showing a steady increase in unique clients year-over-year.

# Key Recommendations
Seasonal Strategy: Increase inventory and marketing efforts in Q1 (March) and Q4 (November/December) to capitalize on historical sales peaks.

Customer Loyalty: Focus retention efforts on the "Top 5" high-profit customers to sustain long-term revenue.

Product Focus: Prioritize marketing for high-performing sub-categories like Phones and Chairs.
